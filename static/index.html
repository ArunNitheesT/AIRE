02 — Framework

Three possible verdicts

Every audit returns one of three structured verdicts, each with a clear rationale and actionable guidance.

Verdict A

Do Not Automate

The task is related to life safety, legal judgment, medical decisions, or emotional care. Human authority is non-negotiable. Automation is not acceptable due to ethical or legal risk.

Verdict B

Human-in-the-Loop

The task is appropriate for AI support, but a qualified human must validate every decision. AI drives speed; humans maintain accountability.

Verdict C

Fully Automate

The task is routine, risk-free, and has the structural form that is best automated. Human review is not necessary. Efficiency benefits are possible.
